About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

We are the country’s most celebrated restaurants; the creation of leading Assam's restaurant group.

The restaurant is an organic space reflective of nature inspired cuisine. The interplay of textures and colour brings life and a vibrance that embraces the restaurant’s place in the dress circle of Jorhat. An ode to the assam's landscape, from the vast nature floor, to the cracked bark of a paperbark tree, every detail from the ground up has been thoughtfully considered.
